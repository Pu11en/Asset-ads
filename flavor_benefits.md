## Mango Passion
**Mango + Passion Fruit**

- **Mango:** High in Vitamin A (vision, skin, immune), Vitamin C, folate. Contains digestive enzymes (amylases). Natural sweetness, no added sugar.
- **Passion Fruit:** Rich in fiber, Vitamin C, Vitamin A. Contains antioxidants (beta-carotene). Supports immune system, aids digestion.

## Mauby
**Mauby (Colubrina elliptica)**

- Traditional Caribbean bark drink. Used for centuries in the Caribbean for digestion and appetite. Natural bitter compounds support liver function and metabolism. Refreshing, slightly bitter, acquired taste.

## Peanut Punch
**Peanut + Dairy (optional) / Nutritious Energy Drink**

- **Peanuts:** High in healthy fats, protein, Vitamin E, B Vitamins (niacin, folate), magnesium, potassium. Natural energy booster. Supports brain function and heart health.
- Caribbean staple — peanut punch has been a go-to energy drink long before energy drinks existed.
